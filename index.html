Clips Uploader

A simple service for content creators to upload short video clips to TikTok.

What it does

  • Upload short-form video clips to your TikTok account
  • Manage video metadata — titles, descriptions, and tags
  • Schedule and organize your content publishing

How it works

Clips Uploader connects to your TikTok account via the official TikTok Content Posting API. You authorize the app once, then use it to publish your video clips directly to TikTok.

Your data

We only store OAuth tokens and video metadata locally on your device. We do not collect personal information or use any analytics or tracking services.
